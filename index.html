Privacy Policy

Official Policy for Marco's Limited Applications

At Marco's Limited, we recognize that privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Marco's Limited on the Google Play Store. It explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our services.

By using our Applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to ensuring your data is handled transparently and responsibly.

1. Information Collection & Third-Party SDKs

We do not collect or store personal identity information on our own servers. However, our apps integrate with third-party service providers that may collect information used to identify you (such as Advertising IDs). These include:

  • Google Play Services: For core functionality and security.
  • AdMob: To serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements.
  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): To help us understand app performance and fix technical issues.
  • Facebook: For social integration or advertising features where applicable.

2. GDPR & International Privacy

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), Marco's Limited complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We use a Consent Management Platform (CMP) to ensure you have clear, informed choices regarding your data consent preferences for advertising and analytics.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Because we do not store your personal data on our own infrastructure, we do not have a standard retention period. Data collected by third-party SDKs is managed according to their respective privacy policies. Users may reset their Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads via their Android device settings at any time.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will immediately delete it from our records.

5. Security Measures

We take administrative, physical, and electronic measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

Marco's Limited may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
